# NIFTY 50 Financial Expert Brief

Use this when reading third-party street forecasts (media, brokers, global banks).

## Index vs single stock

- Accept only **NIFTY 50 / Nifty index level** targets (roughly 15,000–35,000 points).
- Reject single-stock targets, Sensex-only outlooks, and F&O strategy articles unless they state an explicit NIFTY index level.

## Horizon language

| Language | Typical mapping |
|----------|-----------------|
| short term / near term / this week | ~5–10 trading days |
| 2–3 weeks / fortnight | ~10–15 days |
| monthly / 1 month | ~20–22 trading days |
| quarterly / 3 months | ~60–65 trading days |
| FY26 / calendar year / by Dec | multi-month — note mismatch vs 14d tab |

Always extract the **article's stated target date** when visible; do not assume tab horizon.

## Factor transmission (India)

- **FII/DII flows** — primary liquidity; FII selling often pressures NIFTY short term; DII SIP flows can absorb.
- **India VIX** — fear gauge; spikes align with drawdowns; use as regime context not sole driver.
- **USD/INR** — short-run co-moves with FII risk appetite; rupee weakness can hurt NIFTY via flows.
- **Oil (Brent)** — India import bill; spikes hurt margins and INR; link varies by regime.
- **US equities / US10y** — global risk appetite; SP500 up often supports EM indices on risk-on days.
- **NIFTY PCR** — high PCR can mean put support or fear; contrarian at extremes.

## Reading broker/media pages

- Listing pages may link older articles — prefer **publish date** near today (India trading calendar).
- Charts and infographics may carry the target — cross-check text and image.
- Global bank views on ET/Moneycontrol topic pages are still **media republished** views — cite the article URL domain.
